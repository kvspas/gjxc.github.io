    Annealing of precision resistance alloy wire and its use process

    Sources：本站 | Release date: 2019-05-25 | Views：177
    Keywords：Annealing of precision resistance alloy wire and its use process
    Wires are available in a variety of conveyor formats, from 80mm to 630mm flange diameter reels and drum/discharge drums or drums. It is important to match the delivery form to the wire diameter and application. We can recommend which one is more suitable for you. Length and payment requirements.

    The resistance wires are typically provided for complete annealing, which are strand annealed in a furnace containing an inert atmosphere, resulting in a wire that is easy to process and free of grease. Brushed lubricants are specifically designed to "flash" in the furnace without leaving traces on the surface. Precision Resistive Alloy Wires also provide "hard-drawn" wire and, for certain sizes, a lubricated surface. If you need more specific mechanical yields, elongation or tensile strength ranges, these can also be produced and tested in our laboratory.

    Repeated heating and cooling of the resistance wire during use can cause thermal stress to enter the wire. Although alloy combinations have been developed for many years and ensuring that they have a low temperature linear expansion coefficient have become critical design effects, these stresses will improve life. In cases where it is difficult to replace the heating element, such as in a heating cable under the floor, it may be helpful to bunch or twist the conductor. Wire stranding and stranded conductors have the same inspection scheme as solid conductors, so conductors will exhibit the same consistency regardless of form.
