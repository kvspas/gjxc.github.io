    Technical department's test and selection recommendations for alloy wire

    Sources：本站 | Release date: 2019-06-19 | Views：167
    Keywords：Technical department's test and selection recommendations for alloy wire
    Measure and record information on diameter, ellipticity, drag, reel length and weight on a centralized computer system, as well as traceability traceable to the parent melt, giving customers peace of mind that their delivery is exactly what they ordered . Mechanical testing was also performed to confirm that the wire has been annealed to the correct mechanical strength, elongation or hardness.

    Some raw material batches have been tested hundreds of times, starting with 2 tons of delivery and completing 5kg reels, so when we say "precision wires end-to-end", we can be sure of what we mean. The use of resistance wires is increasing in automotive applications, and emission control and comfort heating mean that it is a real advantage to be able to manufacture and maintain records in a manner suitable for the industry.

    The technical department can help you determine the wires you need, without being bound by a range of alloys, to accurately suggest the right material, and to get it from many high quality alloy casters. By considering the application, the cross-sectional area, heat transfer line surface area and alloy suitability in each case can be calculated. Using detailed knowledge of the internal resistance calculator and alloy properties, whether you are manufacturing a household electronic heatsink or a resistor shunt for a power circuit, you can recommend alloy selection.
