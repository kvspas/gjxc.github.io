    Company Profile
    • Company Profile
      

    Changzhou Hanyi Import&Export Co., Ltd is in Changzhou city (Dragon city) which has a history of 3000 years and is located in the center of Yangtze rive area.

    The Hanyi will provide customers with good products, technical support and after services. It has subordinate companies like: Changzhou Lucheng Huire Electronics Plant, Changzhou Huanrui Electronics Co., Ltd, Changzhou Yicheng Electric&Machinery Plant and other hundreds of plants. It is engaged in import and export business of all kinds of commodities and technologies like: thermocouple alloy, resistance alloy, high temperature alloy and other alloys; compensating thermocouple wires, compensating cables, high temperature wires and other different kinds of wires&cables; review mirror actuator, folder, micro motor and other auto parts; alloy manufacturing equipment&technology, wire&cable manufacturing equipment, electrical machinery and others; hardware, metallic material, electronics and others. It has good products and technical teams. It has dozens of product patents and participates in the drafting and revision of national standards.

    The Hanyi has made remarkable achievement since it’s establishment. It is a modern company with culture as it’s core value and will work harder to improve and innovate. Company spirit: Loyalty, dedication, responsibility and collaboration. Company rules: Standard, professional, pragmatic, efficient. Company cooperative rules: Sincere, win-win, long-term.

    Service purpose：

    To provide the best quality products with the most reasonable price and the most perfect service; To improve customer’s efficiency and quality according to customer’s requirements.
